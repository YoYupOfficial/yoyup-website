Michael: the funny

Hilarious, strong and better than you. Michael is the best of the Yoyup team!!!

Biography

Michael is the strongest of the Yoyup team with the power of roasting people in his way. Michael is what keeps the Yoyup team entertained with his hilarious jokes. Without Michael, the Yoyup team would fall apart.

Abilities

Michael is able to turn himself to diamond, when he is in diamond form, nothing can stop him as he goes barreling through all his enemies and sends them to jail in an instant.

Fun Facts

  • He has an impressive collection of ancient swords.
  • He enjoys playing chess in his free time.
